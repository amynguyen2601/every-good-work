Semester of Service

Animal Shelter Volunteer

Volunteering at an animal shelter is a fantastic way for kids to learn about responsibility, compassion, and the importance of caring for animals in need.

These programs not only benefit the shelter and its residents but also contribute to the personal growth of young volunteers. We'll explore a variety of engaging activities suitable for kids at an animal shelter.

Stop Hunger Volunteer

Food banks play a crucial role in addressing hunger and providing essential support to communities in need.

Behind the scenes, a dedicated army of volunteers, including children and teenagers, contributes to the success of these initiatives. We'll explore the meaningful experiences that await young volunteers in the world of food banks.

Weekend of Good

By integrating service into their weekend, students not only enrich their academic experience but also foster a sense of civic responsibility, leadership, and a deeper connection to the communities they serve.
